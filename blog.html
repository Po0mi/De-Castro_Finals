🪲 Debugging – Battle of the Bugs

May 10, 2025 – Every coder faces these wild foes. Here’s how to survive the swarm.

🧭 Tip 1: Read error messages—they're cryptic, but full of clues.
🔍 Tip 2: Use `console.log()` like a torch in the dark.
🧱 Tip 3: Break your code into small parts.
🧠 Tip 4: Step away and come back—fresh eyes win battles.
🧘 Tip 5: Celebrate every bug you defeat. XP gained!
